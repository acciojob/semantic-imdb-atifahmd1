Summary

The Shawshank Redemption is a story of hope and perseverance. The film follows Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly convicted of murder, and his friendship with Red, a fellow inmate. Set in Shawshank Prison, it explores themes of redemption, resilience, and the human spirit's capacity to endure.
